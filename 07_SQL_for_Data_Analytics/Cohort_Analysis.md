# Cohort Analysis

## Overview

Cohort Analysis groups customers based on a shared characteristic, such as their first purchase month.

It helps businesses understand customer behavior over time.

---

## Why It Matters

Data Analysts use Cohort Analysis to:

- Measure retention
- Analyze customer lifecycle
- Track engagement trends
- Evaluate marketing effectiveness

---

## Example Cohort

| Customer | First Purchase Month |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Rahul | Jan 2025 |
| Priya | Jan 2025 |
| Aman | Feb 2025 |

Customers from the same first-purchase month belong to the same cohort.

---

## Business Questions

1. Which cohort has the highest retention?
2. Which marketing campaign acquired the best customers?
3. How does engagement change over time?

---

## Key Takeaways

- Cohorts help analyze customer behavior.
- Commonly used in SaaS and e-commerce.
- Supports retention analysis.
